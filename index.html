Discover new possibilities with Sober — it's much more than just a sobriety counter!

Sober not only allows you to track alcohol-free days but also helps you recall your personal reasons for choosing a sober lifestyle, maintain a detailed record of alcohol consumption, calculate the economic benefits of quitting, and, of course, celebrate your successes and achievements.

Sober will become your reliable assistant in reducing or completely abstaining from alcohol.

Here's what you can do with our app:

  • Track the number of sober days.
  • Remind yourself of personal reasons to stay sober.
  • Keep a detailed account of alcohol consumption for dynamic analysis.
  • Receive rewards and achievements for sober days.
  • Find out how much you save by giving up alcohol.
  • Get tips for easing hangovers.
  • Learn about the body's recovery process after quitting alcohol.

We sincerely hope that Sober will become a reliable companion on your journey to a sober life and help you overcome dependency.
